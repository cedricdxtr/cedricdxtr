### 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a developer and multimedia artist based in the **Philippines**, with a degree in **Information Technology and Multimedia Arts & Animation**. I like living at the intersection of **code and visuals** — building interactive software while bringing things to life through motion, 3D, and design.

- 🔭 Currently building interactive/XR & VR projects and game prototypes
- 🎮 Interests: game development, VR/XR, 3D animation, systems tooling
- 🧩 Background: QA, multimedia production, and technical documentation (internship experience)
- 🌱 Always exploring the overlap between **engineering** and **motion design**
